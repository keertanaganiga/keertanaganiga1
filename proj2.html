RARE BLOOD TRACKER ANDROID APPLICATION

There are lots of factors to be considered while taking the blood from any donor using an application. Personal information, last donation date and any reaction occurred, etc. all have to be stored. These stored information is possible to access with the different techniques of data mining like classification, clustering, prediction, association, etc which gives the segments of potential blood donors in terms of identifiable characteristics, behaviour patterns, and it also suggest properly at the time of blood requirement in urgent cases.

There is communication gap between patients, donors, blood banks and hospitals that need blood in case of an emergency. Medical facility is not available equally in every part of the country. If someone needs blood, first of all he searches it within his family members, then nearest hospitals and blood banks. If they cannot manage blood in these ways, it is really hard for them to contact other people to collect blood in a short time.

This project plays an important role in saving lives, which is its main aim. The Rare Blood Tracker is developed to help people to find the nearest blood bank to which they can donate the blood and request the blood from. Application consists of a login and register page for users as well as for blood banks which helps them to register and login in with the application. All the necessary details of users is entered at the time of registration which can later be updated. In case of emergencies, rather than searching for a donor application searches for the blood bank with available blood group. This is considered as a reliable method compared to directly contacting a donor without any medical information. This application not only connects the user and blood banks, but also acts as a bridge between blood banks in case of unavailability of rare blood types. The main aim of developing this application is to reduce the search and increase the availability of rare blood types
